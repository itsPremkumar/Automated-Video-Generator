### What was removed for this project's use

To keep the vendored copy clean, zero-cost, and commercially safe, the following
were stripped during vendoring:

- Voicebox Cloud sync (`routes/cloud.py`, `services/cloud.py`)
- HumeAI TADA paid/cloud backend (`backends/hume_backend.py`)
- ROCm / CUDA GPU-binary auto-updaters
- Upstream test suite and `__pycache__`
